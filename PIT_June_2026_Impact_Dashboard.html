Executive Summary

  • 85% Showed Improvement: 17/20 candidates improved conversion vs May. Even marginal gainers like Sachin (+3%) and Mohan Reddy (+10%) delivered strong revenue jumps (₹0.58L and ₹1.51L).
  • 55% Crossed 25% Benchmark: 11 candidates met the official PIT success threshold. Arjun led at 168%, followed by Swati (105%) and Ravi (100%).
  • ₹5.57L Revenue Growth (+12.4%): ₹44.99L (May) → ₹50.56L (June). 15/20 grew revenue individually. Surplus from growers = ₹7.27L.
  • Floor Outperformance: 10/17 (59%) now earn above floor average. May's bottom performers → June's outperformers.
  • 3 Conv Winners Lost Revenue: Ravi (+100% conv, –₹0.34L), Amita (+25%, –₹0.10L), Isha (+56%, flat). Needs lead allocation review.
  • 3 Declined: Himanshu (–31%, –₹1.08L) is biggest risk. Nitesh (–22%) and Kuldeep (–3%) also declined.
  • 3–6M Tenure = Sweet Spot: 62% success rate, 50.8% avg improvement — highest ROI window.
